Foraging Islands

The Forest

Here, you get oak wood, and if you stand at the edges of the forest, next to the Graveyard, the zombies will attack you. There is a small hut in the middle of the forest, and there is an NPC selling stuff.

The Park

Here, you collect every other variation of wood, and fish. You can also get a talisman here by completing the race. Once you complete the Melancholic Viking's Quest, he will open shop for five minutes. He sells The Raiders' Axe, wich is a good mid-game weapons, and other items.
